What is the bag policy?

Guests and their belongings are subject to search upon entry. You can help keep the lines moving quickly by leaving large bags at home. NO backpacks, purses or bags are allowed to be brought into the venue, EXCEPT for the following:

  • Bags that are clear plastic, vinyl or PVC and do not exceed 12" x 6" x 12".
  • One-gallon clear plastic freezer bags, i.e. Ziploc bag or something similar;
  • Small clutch bags, approximately the size of a hand (and that do not exceed 4.5" x 6.5‚Äù), with or without a handle or strap; and
  • Clear plastic Hydration packs (empty upon entry), i.e. clear plastic CamelBak bag or something similar.
  • For the avoidance of doubt, iridescent, color-tinted, and opalescent bags are NOT allowed; they must be clear.

What ADA and Accessibility accommodations are available at Smokin' Grooves Festival?

Wheelchair Accessibility The venue is fully navigable for people with mobility disabilities. The event is held on a flat paved surface. We encourage all guests with mobility disabilities to utilize personal forms of transportation, including wheelchairs and scooters. If you need to recharge your motorized wheelchair, you may do so at the ADA kiosk. Patrons should bring all cords and accessories needed to charge their devices. Chargers must use a 110-volt, 20amp circuit. We will not be providing wheelchair or scooter rentals.

  • For any accessibility-related questions please email us at access@smokingroovesfestival.com.
  • There will be no parking on site at the festival grounds. However, there are a number of paid parking lots close to the park.
  • There will be an ADA entry lane/entrance, located at the main entrance. Disabled guests may use this lane/entrance to safely enter the venue.After using ADA entry, please visit the ADA kiosk immediately after so that you can keep using ADA entry for the following show days.
